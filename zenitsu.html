Zenitsu Agatsuma

Demon Slayer

About

Zenitsu Agatsuma is a Demon Slayer and one of the main characters in Demon Slayer. He is a user of Thunder Breathing and fights alongside Tanjiro and Inosuke. Unlike his brave companions, Zenitsu is extremely cowardly and often cries and complains, but he has hidden potential that emerges in battle.

Personality

Zenitsu is timid, nervous, and constantly scared. He often complains and cries, especially when faced with danger. However, when he falls asleep or is in danger, his true combat abilities surface. He has a kind heart and genuinely cares about his friends, especially his mentor Kaigaku.

Plot

The story surrounding Zenitsu Agatsuma in Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba follows a fearful and often comedic young swordsman who becomes a member of the Demon Slayer Corps. Zenitsu initially appears cowardly, frequently panicking and doubting himself in dangerous situations. However, despite his anxiety, he possesses exceptional talent in swordsmanship—specifically mastering the Thunder Breathing technique. Interestingly, his true strength only emerges when he loses consciousness or falls asleep, allowing him to fight with incredible speed and precision. Throughout the story, Zenitsu travels alongside Tanjiro Kamado and Inosuke Hashibira, helping battle powerful demons while gradually building courage. His personal arc focuses on overcoming fear, gaining confidence, and learning to stand his ground while awake. Zenitsu’s journey blends humor and growth—showing that even someone overwhelmed by fear can become strong and reliable when it truly matters.

Abilities

  • Thunder Breathing: Sword style using lightning-based attacks
  • Thunderclap and Flash: Lightning-fast strike technique
  • Sleep Combat: Can fight while unconscious using muscle memory
  • Enhanced Hearing: Can detect demons and predict movements
  • Sixth Form: Thunder Breathing's most powerful form
