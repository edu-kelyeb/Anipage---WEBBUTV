Goku

Dragon Ball

About

Goku is a Saiyan warrior and the main protagonist of the Dragon Ball series. Originally sent to Earth as an infant, he was adopted by Grandpa Gohan and grew up as a humble martial artist. He spends his days training, competing in tournaments, and defending Earth from powerful enemies.

Abilities

  • Ki Manipulation: Energy manipulation for attacks and flight
  • Kamehameha: His signature energy wave attack
  • Super Saiyan: Transforms into a golden-haired warrior
  • Ultra Instinct: Mastered technique allowing automatic dodging
  • Spirit Bomb: Massive energy sphere gathered from all lifeforms

Personality

Goku is pure-hearted, innocent, and always eager for a good fight. He is humble despite his immense power and believes in giving everyone a fair chance. His love for training and martial arts is unmatched, and he always pushes himself to become stronger.
