Tanjiro Kamado

Demon Slayer

About

Tanjiro is the main protagonist of Demon Slayer. He was a coal seller who lived happily with his family until a demon attack killed most of them and turned his sister Nezuko into a demon. He joined the Demon Slayer Corps to find a cure for Nezuko and avenge his family.

Personality

Tanjiro is incredibly kind-hearted and always sees the good in people, even demons. He is patient, hardworking, and never gives up. His determination to save his sister and protect others drives him to become stronger.

Plot

Before destiny ever called his name, the mountains already knew Tanjiro Kamado—a boy whose kindness was as steady as the falling snow. In Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba, his story begins not with ambition, but with loss. After returning home one winter day, Tanjiro finds his family slaughtered by demons, with only his sister Nezuko Kamado left alive—transformed into a demon herself. Refusing to give up on her humanity, Tanjiro sets out on a journey to find a cure and avenge his family. Along the way, he trains as a Demon Slayer, mastering Water Breathing techniques and developing a sharp sense of smell that helps him track enemies and read emotions. As he travels, Tanjiro encounters powerful allies and deadly foes, gradually uncovering the dark hierarchy of demons led by Muzan Kibutsuji. Despite the violence surrounding him, he remains compassionate—even toward his enemies—often recognizing the tragedy behind their existence. His story is one of resilience and empathy: a path shaped not just by vengeance, but by an unwavering determination to protect others and save what remains of his family.

Abilities

  • Water Breathing: Sword style using water-based attacks
  • Hinokami Kagura: Ancient dance technique passed down in his family
  • Total Concentration: Maximizes breathing for enhanced physical abilities
  • Enhanced Sense of Smell: Can detect demons and their weaknesses
  • Sun Breathing: The original breathing style, most powerful
