Levi Ackerman

Attack on Titan

About

Levi Ackerman is a captain in the Survey Corps and is widely recognized as humanity's strongest soldier. Despite his short stature, his combat abilities are unmatched. He is a master of the 3D Maneuver Gear and has killed more Titans than any other soldier. He is also the uncle of Mikasa Ackerman.

Personality

Levi is stoic, serious, and pragmatic. He has a short temper and values cleanliness and order. Despite his harsh exterior, he deeply cares about his comrades and will do anything to protect them. He is known for his catchphrase "I'll kill you" and his unwavering determination to eradicate all Titans.

Plot

The story surrounding Levi Ackerman in Attack on Titan focuses on one of humanity’s strongest soldiers in a world where giant humanoid creatures called Titans threaten human survival. Levi is a captain in the Survey Corps, an elite military group that ventures outside the walls to fight Titans and uncover the truth about their existence. Known for his unmatched combat skill and cold, disciplined personality, he plays a key role in many of the series’ most dangerous missions. Despite his harsh demeanor, Levi deeply cares for his comrades and carries the emotional weight of losing many of them in battle. As the story unfolds, Levi becomes entangled in larger political and existential conflicts, including conspiracies within the human government and the true origins of the Titans. His personal arc also explores his past—growing up in poverty in the underground city—and his loyalty to his mentor, Erwin Smith. Levi’s role is less about being the central protagonist and more about being a stabilizing force: a relentless fighter who embodies humanity’s struggle for survival, while grappling with the cost of war and the burden of leadership.
