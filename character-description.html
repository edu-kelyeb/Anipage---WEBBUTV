Character Descriptions

Monkey D. Luffy (One Piece)

Luffy is the captain of the Straw Hat Pirates and dreams of becoming the Pirate King. He has eaten the Gum-Gum Fruit, giving him rubber-like abilities.

Naruto Uzumaki (Naruto)

Naruto is a ninja who seeks recognition and dreams of becoming Hokage. He has the Nine-Tails Fox sealed inside him.

Ichigo Kurosaki (Bleach)

Ichigo is a Soul Reaper who can see ghosts and fights Hollows. He becomes a substitute Soul Reaper after gaining powers from Rukia. He is known for his orange hair and strong sense of justice.

Giorno Giovanna (JoJo's Bizarre Adventure)

Giorno is the protagonist of part 5, "Vento Aureo". He is the son of Dio Brando and has the stand "Gold Experience", whcih allows him to create life from inanimate object.

Goku (Dragon Ball)

Goku is a Saiyan warrior and the main protagonist of the Dragon Ball series. He spends his days training martial arts and entering tournaments to become stronger.

Tanjiro Kamado (Demon Slayer)

Tanjiro is a kind-hearted boy who becomes a Demon Slayer after his family is attacked by demons and his sister Nezuko is turned into a demon.

Light Yagami (Death Note)

Light is a brilliant high school student who finds a supernatural notebook that can kill anyone whose name is written in it.

Edward Elric (Fullmetal Alchemist)

Edward is the Fullmetal Alchemist, the youngest state alchemist in history. He and his brother Alphonse search for the Philosopher's Stone to restore their bodies.

Satoru Gojo (Jujutsu Kaisen)

Gojo is a powerful Jujutsu Sorcerer and teacher at Tokyo Prefectural Jujutsu High School. He is known as the strongest sorcerer in the world.

Izuku Midoriya (My Hero Academia)

Midoriya, also known as Deku, is the main protagonist of My Hero Academia. He was born without powers in a world where everyone has them, but inherits the strongest quirk from his idol All Might.

Zenitsu Agatsuma (Demon Slayer)

Zenitsu is a Demon Slayer who uses Thunder Breathing. Despite being cowardly, he has incredible speed and is a skilled swordsman.

Levi Ackerman (Attack on Titan)

Levi is humanity's strongest soldier and captain of the Survey Corps. He is a master of the 3D Maneuver Gear and one of the best fighters in the world.
