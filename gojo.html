Satoru Gojo

Jujutsu Kaisen

About

Satoru Gojo is a Jujutsu Sorcerer and one of the main characters in Jujutsu Kaisen. He serves as a teacher at Tokyo Prefectural Jujutsu High School and is widely recognized as the strongest Jujutsu Sorcerer in the world. He is a member of the Gojo clan, one of the most prestigious families in the jujutsu world.

Personality

Satoru Gojo has a personality that mixes confidence, playfulness, and hidden seriousness. On the surface, Gojo is very **carefree and goofy**. He jokes around a lot, teases his students, and doesn’t always act like a typical serious teacher. He often comes off as cocky because he knows he’s the strongest. Underneath that, he is extremely **confident and self-assured**. His power makes him feel almost untouchable, and he rarely shows fear. This confidence can look like arrogance, but it’s backed up by his actual ability. However, Gojo is also **thoughtful and strategic**. He understands that the jujutsu world is flawed and wants to change it. Instead of doing everything alone, he chooses to teach and raise a new generation of stronger, smarter sorcerers. There’s also a more **serious and lonely side** to him. Being the strongest means very few people truly understand him, and he carries the burden of protecting others by himself. This is why he puts so much importance on his students—he doesn’t want them to feel the same isolation. In short, Gojo is playful on the outside, but deeply responsible and purposeful underneath, with a mix of confidence, care, and quiet loneliness.

Plot

Satoru Gojo is the strongest jujutsu sorcerer, and he plays a major role as both a fighter and a teacher. He protects people from powerful curses and works at Jujutsu High, where he trains students to become strong enough to change the corrupt jujutsu system. Because Gojo is so powerful, most enemies cannot act freely while he is around. This makes him the biggest obstacle for curses and villains. To deal with this, they create a careful plan to remove him from the picture instead of fighting him directly. At a major point in the story, they succeed in sealing Gojo away. Once he is gone, the balance of power breaks. Strong curses begin causing more destruction, and the world becomes much more dangerous. Without Gojo’s protection, his students and other sorcerers are forced to step up. They must fight stronger enemies, face harsher situations, and grow quickly in order to survive and carry on his goal of changing the system.

Abilities

  • Six Eyes: Allows him to see cursed energy in detail
  • Infinity: Creates a barrier that slows down anything approaching
  • Domain Expansion: Creates a space where all attacks are guaranteed to hit
  • Blue: Attracts and amplifies anything in a vacuum
  • Red: Pushes and repels with opposite force of Blue
  • Purple: Combines Blue and Red for an unstoppable attack

Personality

Gojo is laid-back, casual, and often appears carefree. He has a playful personality and loves teasing his students and colleagues. However, he takes his role as a teacher and protector seriously and will do anything to protect the younger generation of sorcerers.
