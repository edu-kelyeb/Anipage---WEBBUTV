Tanjiro Kamado

Demon Slayer

About

Tanjiro is the main protagonist of Demon Slayer. He was a coal seller who lived happily with his family until a demon attack killed most of them and turned his sister Nezuko into a demon. He joined the Demon Slayer Corps to find a cure for Nezuko and avenge his family.

Abilities

  • Water Breathing: Sword style using water-based attacks
  • Hinokami Kagura: Ancient dance technique passed down in his family
  • Total Concentration: Maximizes breathing for enhanced physical abilities
  • Enhanced Sense of Smell: Can detect demons and their weaknesses
  • Sun Breathing: The original breathing style, most powerful

Personality

Tanjiro is incredibly kind-hearted and always sees the good in people, even demons. He is patient, hardworking, and never gives up. His determination to save his sister and protect others drives him to become stronger.
